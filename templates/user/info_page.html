Gas stations risk mapping survey

The survey is conducted as part of a joint British-Ukrainian project (more details) within the partnership between Odesa Polytechnic National University and Portsmouth University.

The purpose of the survey: To rank the economic, social and environmental vulnerabilities of gas stations depending on such common emergency scenarios as an explosion with a shock wave, fire of oil products spill and a «Fireball».

The project is extremely important for the safety of residents of any region.
