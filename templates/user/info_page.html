Gas stations risk mapping survey

The survey is conducted as part of a joint British-Ukrainian project (more details) within the partnership between Odesa Polytechnic National University and Portsmouth University.

The purpose of the survey: To rank the economic, social and environmental vulnerabilities of petrol stations depending on such common emergency scenarios as an explosion with a shock wave, an oil spill burning and a «fireball» explosion.
